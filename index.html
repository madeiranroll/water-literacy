THE UNRULY WAY

This isn't a method.

It's a middle finger to toxins, served with a cup of bone broth and zero apology.

01
Food is medicine.

I eat fermented everything. Grass-fed protein. Weeds from the meadow. I know the farmers by name. My wallet votes like a wrecking ball.

02
Nature is the first pharmacy.

Sunlight. Grounding. Living water. Circadian and seasonal rhythms — not as a trend, but as a rebellion against the fake-lit, fake-fed world.

03
One ingredient is enough.

Seed oils? Trans fats? Refined sugar, Frankenfoods, plastics, petrochemicals? Zero tolerance. One swap at a time. No rush. No mercy.

04
Soil health = human health.

No healthy food without healthy soil. Regenerative agriculture isn't a buzzword. It's my line in the sand.

05
I listen to my body without shame.

Yes, I'm that annoyingly picky friend. I bring my own salt to restaurants and raw milk to cafés. I used to apologize. I don't anymore.

06
I detox gently. Then I go deeper.

Bone broths, bitter herbs, then concentrated extracts. Soft first. Then fierce.

07
Conscious resilience.

Stress and fear are toxins too. If I fail today? I still pat myself on the back. I'm not afraid of what I know. I'm armed with it.

08
The body is infinite possibility.

Trampolines. Crushed bones for toothpaste. Resin chewing. Aluminum foil at night. Chakras. Yes, even the really woo stuff. Nothing is too much when you love your body like a rebel loves her cause.
